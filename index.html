      Biography

      I am currently a Research Scientist at Solaya, where I am working on novel-view synthesis and material appearance modeling.

      Previously, I completed a postdoc in the GraphDeco Team at INRIA Sophia-Antipolis, supervised by George Drettakis and Adrien Bousseau. I hold a PhD in Computer Graphics from Télécom Paris, where I was supervised by Tamy Boubekeur and Jean-Marc Thiery, (Title: “Morphing and level-of-detail operators for interactive digital material design and rendering”)

      During my PhD, I interned twice at Adobe Research Paris (Summer 2021 and Autumn 2022). I hold a Master’s Degree from Télécom Paris and have interned at Dassault Systèmes working with realtime path-tracing on the GPU using a material graph.

      My research interests include:

      • material appearance modeling
      • real-time rendering
      • novel-view synthesis
      • deep generative models.
